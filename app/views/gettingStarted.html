Getting started

Things you need to set up

Gmail

The user experience team use gmail as well as the official DWP email.

Set up a gmail account in this format: {yourname}.digitaldwp@gmail.com.

Trello

Most projects and the user experience team use Trello to track work and tasks.

Set up an account at www.trello.com, using your gmail account. You'll be invited or added to Trello boards.

Design community

The DWP user experience team is part of a wider design community in government, centred around GDS.

Your line manager will get you added to the digital service designers email list.

Slack

The DWP user experience community and the Government-wide design community use slack instant messaging to ask questions and share ideas.

Email colinoakley.digitaldwp@gmail.com from your gmail and ask him to add you to the digitalDWP slack channel.

There are particular channels for each area - join at least the content and general channels.

You can also join the ukgovernmentdigital.slack.com channel using your DWP email when you have it.

Your timesheet

Make your own copy of this DWP timesheet and add it to your google drive - update this at least weekly, ideally every day.

Your bank details

You need to put your own bank details into 'RM' - the internal staff system. To do this you'll need a DWP machine and your smartcard. You can't be paid until you've done this so don't forget.

A business travel account

Email cd.businesstravelpolicyteam@dwp.gsi.gov.uk from your gsi email (you'll need a DWP machine). Include your staff number, and ask them to set up a www.trips.uk.com account - this is where you'll book work travel and accommodation

Social media

Many of the team tweet and blog about the work we do. You may like to follow these twitter accounts for a start:

  • @BenHolliday - head of user experience
  • @kevincunnington - head of Business Transformation
  • @DigitalDWP
  • @GDSteam
